The 1st Sustainable Development goal

Organizations that help achieve the goal

There are many organisations that are helping the world to achieve the 17 SDG. Those mainly include the United Nations, Greenpeace and many small charities. Also, there exists an organization that is responsible for reducing poverty and promoting sustainable development. It is the United Nations Development Programme, or UNDP.

The organisations' websites all have suggestions on how we can help the UN with achieving the SD goals. I encourage you to look at some of them so that we can all help to end poverty
